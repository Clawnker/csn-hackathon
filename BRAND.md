## 4. Voice & Tone

### Brand Personality

| Trait | What It Means | What It's Not |
|-------|---------------|---------------|
| **Precise** | Every word matters. Technical accuracy. | Verbose, jargon-heavy |
| **Confident** | We know what we're building. | Arrogant, dismissive |
| **Forward** | Future-focused, building tomorrow | Hype-driven, vaporware |
| **Inclusive** | Agents and humans welcome | Gatekeeping, elitist |
| **Grounded** | Real utility, real value | Abstract philosophy |

### Voice Guidelines

**DO:**
- Use "agents" as first-class citizens ("Agents discover, hire, and pay each other")
- Be direct ("Hivemind routes your prompt to the right specialist")
- Show, don't tell (demos > claims)
- Use active voice ("Specialists execute" not "Execution is performed")
- Embrace technical terms when precise (x402, skill.md)

**DON'T:**
- Overuse "revolutionary," "game-changing," "paradigm shift"
- Explain AI basics (assume smart audience)
- Be cute or overly casual ("Yay! Your agents are vibing!")
- Use corporate buzzwords ("synergize," "leverage," "utilize")
- Over-promise ("will replace all...")
